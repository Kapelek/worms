HOW TO START?

At first, choose the colors of your worms (required) and their names.
You can set the score goal also.

Each player has diffrent keys to control the worm left-right.
You can see them under 'KEYBINDINGS' sign.

After clicking 'PLAY' button the game will start.

GAME RULES

Players will compete against each other trying not to hit the tail of any worm (including their own) or the yellow border!

The alive players get 1 point from every worm's death. If only one player has left, a new round begins.

The game continues up to the player reaches the score goal with the two-points lead.
